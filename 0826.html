• Tiket pulang-pergi (wajib)
• Konfirmasi hotel
• Visit Japan Web
Transportation Pass
• JR Pass: 7 hari ¥50,000 / 14 hari ¥80,000
• Tokyo Metro Pass: 24 jam ¥800 / 72 jam ¥1,500
